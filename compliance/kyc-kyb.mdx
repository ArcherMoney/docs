Archer requires identity verification before you can send payouts. Individual users complete KYC (Know Your Customer); business accounts complete KYB (Know Your Business). Verification is a one-time process per account and is required to comply with regulations that govern financial transfers.

## Why verification is required

Archer facilitates real-money transfers on behalf of its users. To do this legally and responsibly, Archer must confirm the identity of account holders before allowing payouts to be sent. This protects both you and your recipients by ensuring that funds are only disbursed through verified, legitimate accounts.

<Warning>
  Payouts may be blocked until your verification is approved. If you attempt to send a payout before your account is verified, the transaction will not be processed.
</Warning>

## KYC — Individual verification

KYC applies to individual user accounts. During verification, Archer guides you through a **Persona** inquiry flow embedded in the dashboard.

You will typically need to provide:

- A government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
- A selfie for liveness and identity matching

Persona handles the document capture and review process securely. You do not need to leave the Archer dashboard to complete the flow.

## KYB — Business verification

KYB applies to business accounts. You will be asked to provide:

- **Company name and registration details** — legal business name and jurisdiction of incorporation
- **Beneficial owner information** — identity details for individuals who own or control the business

Like KYC, business verification is powered by **Persona** and is conducted through a guided flow within the dashboard.

## Verification status

You can check your current verification status from the compliance section of your Archer account. Statuses are:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pending">
    Your submission has been received and is under review. This typically takes a short time, but may vary depending on document quality and case complexity. No action is required from you unless Persona requests additional information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Approved">
    Your identity has been verified. You can send payouts without restriction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Failed">
    Verification could not be completed. This may happen if the submitted documents are unclear, expired, or do not match. Contact Archer support for next steps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
