## How Archer works

Archer combines a web dashboard with an on-chain smart contract to give you complete control over automated payouts — with enforceable spending limits and a full transaction audit trail.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up and Archer automatically provisions a smart wallet for you. This is the wallet that sends payouts on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set spending limits">
    Configure per-transaction and per-period maximums. Limits are enforced on-chain by the ArcherAgentEndpoint contract — not just at the application layer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Add recipients by email. Once they complete onboarding, their wallet address is registered on-chain as an approved recipient.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and run payouts">
    Set up event-driven payouts (triggered by conditions) or scheduled payouts (triggered by time). Archer executes them automatically within your defined limits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
