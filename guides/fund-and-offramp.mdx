Archer holds your payout funds in a smart wallet — a blockchain-based account that the Archer agent draws from when executing payouts. The Dashboard gives you a real-time view of that balance and provides controls to add funds or move money back to a traditional bank account.

## View your balance

The Dashboard displays your current **USDC balance** in your payout smart wallet as the first item on the page. The balance is read directly from the blockchain and reflects funds that are available for payouts right now.

## Add funds (on-ramp)

To top up your smart wallet:

1. Click **Add money** on the Dashboard.
2. Connect your bank account using the Plaid integration that appears. Plaid securely links your bank without sharing your credentials with Archer.
3. Select the account and amount you want to transfer. The funds will be pulled via ACH debit and deposited into your smart wallet once the transfer settles.

## ACH off-ramp

To move funds from your smart wallet to a bank account via ACH:

1. Click **ACH Off-ramp** on the Dashboard. A form modal will open.
2. Fill in the recipient details:
   - **Recipient Name** — the name on the destination bank account
   - **Account Number** — the bank account number
   - **Routing Number** — the 9-digit ABA routing number
   - **Amount (USD)** — the amount to transfer
3. Click **Send ACH Payment**. Archer initiates the transfer and returns a payment order confirmation.

## Wire off-ramp

To move funds via wire transfer instead of ACH:

1. Click **Wire Off-ramp** on the Dashboard. The same form modal will open.
2. Enter the same recipient details — recipient name, account number, routing number, and amount.
3. Click **Send Wire Payment**. Archer initiates the wire and returns a payment order confirmation.

<Note>
  Off-ramp transactions — both ACH and wire — may take 1–3 business days to settle depending on your bank and the payment network. The status shown in Archer reflects when Archer submitted the payment order, not when your bank posts the funds.
</Note>
