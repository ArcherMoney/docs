Archer is a blockchain-powered incentive platform that lets you automate programmable USDC payouts to your team members. Instead of manually sending payments each time a goal is hit or a pay period ends, you define the rules once — and Archer executes them automatically, with every transaction enforced on-chain for a full audit trail.

## What Archer does

Archer brings together a web dashboard and smart contract infrastructure so you can design, control, and run incentive programs at any scale. Here's what you can do:

**Event-driven payouts.** Create payouts that trigger when a condition is met — a sales target hit, a milestone reached — Archer monitors conditions and executes payouts automatically within your defined limits.

**On-chain paying limits.** Set maximum amounts per payment, per period, and per number of transactions. These limits aren't just application-level guardrails — they're enforced by the smart contract, so no payout can exceed what you've authorized regardless of how it's triggered.

**Team member management.** Invite recipients by email. Once they complete onboarding and register their wallet, they're ready to receive payouts. You control who is active and can adjust access at any time.

**KYC and KYB compliance.** Before sending payouts, Archer requires identity verification — Know Your Customer (KYC) for individuals and Know Your Business (KYB) for organizations. This keeps your program compliant and your records clean.

<Note>
  All payouts run on the Base network and are executed through your Archer smart wallet. You do not need to manage private keys — your smart wallet is provisioned automatically when you sign in.
</Note>
